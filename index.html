About me

Who Am I?

About me

I'm a student in communication design at Politecnico di Milano. I'm now finishing my first year of university.

I've always been a passionate, active and creative person and this trades have led me towards the exquisit world of design

Hobbies and passions

I've always been attracted to sports and I practiced gymnastics for nine years, volleyball for two years and I'm now training as a crossfitter.

At the age of eight I started my activity as a gymnast at Robur et Virtus in Villasanta, I then moved to Forti e Liberi Monza and lastly to GAL (Ginnastica Artistica Lissonese) in Lissone.
When I was sixteen, after a back injury and a year of rest, I decided to play volleyball at Ascot Triante. Two years later I decided to give gymnastic a second chance but I injured my knee and decided to stop definitively
All of this led me to Crossfit, a lesser known discipline (in Italy, at least!) that requires a lot of physical and mental strength. By now I've been training in Ironfit Monza for two years

From age eleven to age sixteen I studied piano playing in Liceo Musicale Vincenzo Appiani in Monza. In the meanwhile I fed my music related passion by being a member of a choir for two years. In September 2017 I started a new journey: musical theatre. I joined the troupe Dagli Otto Agli Anta and in January 2018 we debuted with the musical Legally Blonde based on the homonym movie.

I started travelling the world in a camper at the age of seven. I visited many European countries such as Norway, Ireland, England, Spain, Portugal, Belgium, Scotland, France and Germany. The only time I didn't travel by camper was when I visited New York and Washington DC.
